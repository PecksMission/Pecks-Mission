Community: Your Stories Matter Too

As a former medic, I treated patients without truly understanding their experience. Now, as I prepare for and go through brain surgery myself, I'm documenting the journey in real-time. My goal is to help healthcare providers, caregivers, and supporters understand what patients actually go through—so they can connect more deeply with the people they're caring for.
