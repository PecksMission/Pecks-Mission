February 15, 2026

Why I'm Starting This Journey

When I first thought about creating this website, I hesitated. Who am I to share my story? What if people think I'm complaining or seeking attention? But then I remembered all the times I felt alone in doctor's offices, all the nights I spent searching for answers online, wondering if anyone else understood what I was going through.

If sharing my experience with Chiari malformation helps even one person feel less alone, or helps someone recognize symptoms and get diagnosed faster, then it's worth any discomfort I might feel about putting myself out there.

This site isn't about me being an expert—it's about being honest. It's about raising awareness for a condition that too many people suffer with in silence. And it's about faith, hope, and finding purpose even in our struggles.

As it says in Romans 8:28, "And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose." I believe this journey, difficult as it is, has purpose—and sharing it with you is part of that.
