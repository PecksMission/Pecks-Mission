The Mission

Bridging The Gap Between The Chart And The Truth

I spent years caring for patients without fully understanding what they were going through. Then I became the patient. This site exists to close the gap between those two experiences — for providers who want to care better and for patients who want to finally feel understood.

01

For Chiari Patients

You're not crazy. You're not imagining it. You might have been told your whole life that you're clumsy or anxious or a slow developer. You weren't. This is for you.

02

For Healthcare Providers

I've worn the scrubs and the hospital gown. There's a gap between what the chart says and what the patient is going through. I'm documenting both sides in real time.

03

For Loved Ones

Keep knocking on the door. The person inside probably won't ask for help even when they need it most. Your presence matters more than you know.
